Keywords: Schengen Information System (SIS), alert, Germany, Austria, France, Italy
Question: How do I resolve a Schengen Information System (SIS) alert for Germany, Austria, France, or Italy issued by the Border and Immigration Coordination Unit (UCFE)?
Answer: Submit the fee and documents through your legal representative. Resolve as soon as possible to avoid a removal order from Portugal.
* Total Cost: €800
* Required Documents:
  * Schengen Information System (SIS) notification (if available)
  * Passport
  * Tax Identification Number (NIF)
  * Social Security Identification Number (NISS)
  * Employment contract or proof of professional activity
  * Recent social security contribution statements
