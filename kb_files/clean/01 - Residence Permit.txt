Question: What legal remedy can be used to obtain a residence permit appointment when no deadline is respected?
Answer: File a judicial action in court to enforce a legally established scheduling deadline of up to 90 working days for your residence permit appointment. Without this action, no deadline is enforced.

Question: What legal remedy can be used to obtain a renewal appointment for a residence permit when no deadline is respected?
Answer: File a judicial action in court to enforce a 90 working day scheduling deadline for your residence permit renewal appointment. Without this action, no deadline is enforced.
