Keywords: Judicial Action, Legal Action, Court, Normal Immigration Appointment
Question: What documents do I need to file a judicial action in court to schedule a normal immigration appointment?
Answer: You must submit the following documents to your lawyer for filing a judicial action:
* Tax Identification Number (NIF)
* Social Security Identification Number (NISS)
* National Health Service user number (SNS user number), if available
* Last three payslips
* Job contract or proof of self-employment activity
* Certificate of residence from the Parish Council (Junta de Freguesia)
* Legal entry visa or current residence permit, if available
* Expression of Interest (EOI) or proof of 12 months of social security contributions
